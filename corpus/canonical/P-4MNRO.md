::: problem
Let

$$
A=\left[\begin{array}{lll}{0} & {1} & {-2} \\ {1} & {1} & {-3} \\ {1} & {2} & {-4}\end{array}\right] \in M_{3}(\mathbb{C})
$$

(a) Find the Jordan canonical form J of A.

(b) Find an invertible matrix $P$ such that $P\inv AP = J$.

> You should not need to compute $P\inv$.
:::
