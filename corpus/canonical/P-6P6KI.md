::: problem
Let $G$ be a finite group and $\pi_0$, $\pi_1$ be two irreducible representations of $G$. Prove or disprove the following assertion:
$\pi_0$ and $\pi_1$ are equivalent if and only if $\det\pi_0(g)=\det\pi_1(g)$
for all $g\in G$.
:::
