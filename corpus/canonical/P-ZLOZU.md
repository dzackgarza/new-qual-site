::: problem
Let
$$
M=\left(\begin{array}{ll}{a} & {b} \\ {c} & {d}\end{array}\right)
\quad \text{and} \quad 
N=\left(\begin{array}{cc}{x} & {u} \\ {-y} & {-v}\end{array}\right)
$$

over a commutative ring $R$, where $b$ and $x$ are units of $R$.
Prove that
$$
M N=\left(\begin{array}{ll}{0} & {0} \\ {0} & {*}\end{array}\right)
\implies MN = 0
.$$
:::
