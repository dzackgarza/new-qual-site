::: problem
Suppose $f(x)$ and $xf(x)$ are integrable on $\RR$.
Define $F$ by
$$
F(t):=\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f(x) \cos (x t) d x
$$
Show that
$$
F'(t)=-\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} x f(x) \sin (x t) d x.
$$
:::
