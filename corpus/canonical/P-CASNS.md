::: problem
Let $\alpha,\beta$ denote the unique positive
real $5^{\text{th}}$ root of 7 and $4^{\text{th}}$ root of 5,
respectively. Determine the degree of $\mathbb Q(\alpha,\beta)$ over $\mathbb Q$.
:::
