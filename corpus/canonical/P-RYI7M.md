::: problem
Suppose that

-   $f_n, f \in L^1$,
-   $f_n \to f$ almost everywhere, and
-   $\int\left|f_{n}\right| \rightarrow \int|f|$.

Show that $\int f_{n} \rightarrow \int f$
:::
