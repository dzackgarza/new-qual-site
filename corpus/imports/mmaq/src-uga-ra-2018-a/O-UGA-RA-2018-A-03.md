::: problem
Let $f$ be a non-negative measurable function on $[0, 1]$.

Show that
$$
\lim _{p \rightarrow \infty}\left(\int_{[0,1]} f(x)^{p} d x\right)^{\frac{1}{p}}=\|f\|_{\infty}.
$$
:::
