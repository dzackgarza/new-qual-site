::: problem
Let
$$
f_{n}(x):=\frac{x}{1+x^{n}}, \quad x \geq 0.
$$

a.  Show that this sequence converges pointwise and find its limit. Is the convergence uniform on $[0, \infty)$?

b.  Compute
    $$
    \lim _{n \rightarrow \infty} \int_{0}^{\infty} f_{n}(x) d x
    $$
:::
