::: problem
Define
$$
E:=\left\{x \in \mathbb{R}:\left|x-\frac{p}{q}\right|<q^{-3} \text { for infinitely many } p, q \in \mathbb{N}\right\}.
$$

Prove that $m(E) = 0$.
:::
