::: problem
(1) Assume $\displaystyle f(z) = \sum_{n=0}^\infty c_n z^n$
    converges in $|z| < R$. Show that for $r <R$,
    $$\frac{1}{2 \pi} \int_0^{2 \pi} |f(r e^{i \theta})|^2 d \theta =
    \sum_{n=0}^\infty |c_n|^2 r^{2n} \; .$$

(2) Deduce Liouville's theorem from (1).
:::
